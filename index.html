What is INGLU?

INGLU is one of the “FASTEST GROWING GLOBAL YOUTH COMMUNITIES” that helps youth in holistic development of experience by working on the E3 Model, which states that Experience is a combination of Education, Enhancement, and Entertainment.

The 3E Model

Education

Learn through structured programs designed by industry experts.

  • • Live workshops & masterclasses
  • • Internship & certification programs
  • • Practical project-based learning
  • • Mentorship from professionals

Enhancement

Develop personality, leadership, and professional skills.

  • • Communication & confidence building
  • • Leadership & teamwork training
  • • Personal branding & resume growth
  • • Career guidance & networking

Entertainment

Enjoy learning with fun, engagement, and community bonding.

  • • Youth festivals & meetups
  • • Hackathons & competitions
  • • Cultural & social events
  • • Travel, fun activities & networking
